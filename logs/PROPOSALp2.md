## My Project Proposal

**What I'm building:** I am building a tool that analyzes "A Modest Proposal" by Jonathan Swift and changes Old English words to more recent versions.
**Why I chose this:** I had to read this story in school and there are strange spellings that made it a bit more difficult to read and understand.
**Core features:** Read the text file, provide summary statistics about the words use, identify Old English words, replace them with modern versions, and save this as a new text file.
**What I don't know yet:** I am not sure how I will identify the Old English words and how I will be able to match them with their more recent version. I think I will have to have a text file with Old English words or create some kind of dictionary.
